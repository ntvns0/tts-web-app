Rayline Echo

Turn anything into an audiobook.

Rayline Echo turns books, documents, papers, and notes into a private, listenable library you can build over time.

Import PDFs, EPUBs, Markdown, text files, and scanned documents. Process locally, queue long jobs, and keep listening where you left off.
